FAQ

What is KL RADIO ?

Kl radio is Indias only 1 digital radio that acts as an efficient barrier between management and students, operates through an app kl radio and also through the website www.kluradio.com

How is KL RADIO different from others ?

It is a massive, flexible media that has been established for enhancements of skills through various fields it has in it and also provides better interaction for the students with college environment.

What we get from KL Radio ?

Kl Radio updates the kl students and also others with the happenings in and around them throughout the corners of the world

How to get into KL RADIO ?

Kl Radio schedules its selections once for every odd semesters for hiring the radio jockeys and also the crew.
